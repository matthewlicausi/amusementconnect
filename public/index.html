Our Vision And Mission

We're making video and redemption gaming more fun for players through powerful self-service, cashless and Point-Of-Sale hardware.
Cost-effective components coupled with a cloud-based infrastructure lowers the cost of entry for arcades and FEC's of all sizes.

Increasing Customer Loyalty

No waiting on repairs for ticket or token jams, no waiting in line for redemption and a "gamified" arcade experience thrills players and keeps them coming back for more.

Decreasing Operating and Labor Costs

Self-Service redemption machines, purchase kiosks and RFID based token/ticketing give operators the advantage of eliminating token/ticket hard costs and being able to operate nearly labor-free.

Increased Profit Margins

Reduced or no labor costs, a very low system cost, elimination of game downtime due to jams, and elimination of excessive or unneeded inventory all contribute.

Our passion has always been connecting people to fun.
The system we've developed makes this easier than ever!
