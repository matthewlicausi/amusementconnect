What We Do

To create a more enriched experience for players and gamers in a space that is more convenient and offers a social platform to connect with other like-minded players.

No waiting on repairs for ticket or token jams, no waiting in line for redemption and a "gamified" arcade experience thrills players and keeps you coming back for more.

How We Do It

Amusement Connect offers arcade and family entertainment centers a suite of powerful custom hardware and cloud-based sofware components that significantly increase customer convenience, and introduce social media and achievement tracking to the overall gaming experience.

Self-Service Redemption

Cutting edge prize vending technology lets you browse and redeem tickets for a wide variety of fantastic prizes. You can get prizes right from our self-service machine, or have larger prizes mailed to you!

Cash/Cashless Payment Kiosk

Use cash or credit card to load credits on your player card. No waiting in line!

Scan to Play!

Just scan your card at the reader and credits will be deducted from your account and loaded on the game! Any tickets you win are stored on your card too, and you can use your card at any arcade with Amusement Connect equipment!

Web-Based Player Dashboard

Register your card to a player account at one of our kiosks, or from your home computer or mobile device. You can track your ticket and credit balances, achievements and high scores anytime, from anywhere!

Social Gamer Network

Join our player forums to post your achievements, chat w/ friends or learn about awesome contents and tournaments at your favorite Amusement Connect arcades and entertainment centers!
